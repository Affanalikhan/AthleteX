MEDICINE BALL POWER COACH - QUICK START GUIDE
==============================================

STEP 1: TRAIN THE MODEL (For Maximum Accuracy)
-----------------------------------------------
Run this command to train on your Roboflow dataset:

    python train_for_accuracy.py

This will:
- Use your 207 images (145 train / 41 val / 21 test)
- Train for 300 epochs with early stopping
- Apply heavy data augmentation
- Save best model automatically

Training time:
- CPU: 8-12 hours
- GPU: 2-4 hours

You can stop anytime with Ctrl+C - progress is saved!


STEP 2: ANALYZE YOUR VIDEO
---------------------------
After training (or use pretrained models), analyze any video:

    python analyze_video.py your_video.mp4

Supported formats: MP4, MOV, AVI
Max size: Any size (no limit)
